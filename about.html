About

My Story

Stephanie Czapla is a Miami-raised Philadelphia-based graphic designer focusing on branding and illustration, focusing on the big picture including the frame. Her work strives for cohesive boldness by pushing the brand’s limits and engaging with their narratives and personas. From beginning to end, she ponders “what is the story, and are we telling it accurately and proudly?”

In 2017, Stephanie was awarded a BFA in Illustration with a Graphic Design/Business double minor at Moore College of Art and Design in Philadelphia. In 2018, she released a children’s book with a local author called “Gobbles Gives A Turkey and Gets Back a Whole Lot More” that’s based on a true Thanksgiving family tradition about charity and compassion. Her clients include Chestnut Hill College, The Women’s Film Festival, SharpApp, and MFAC Inc. who produced music videos for Maxo Kream and other rising rappers.

When she's not rearranging layouts or forgetting to save her file, she's learning about social science or cryptocurrency, cooking a new or tried and true dish, discovering new music, or singing her heart out at karaoke. Her favorite artists at the moment are black midi, Charli XCX, Frank Ocean, and Underworld.
